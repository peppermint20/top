Honey Walnut Shrimp

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • ⅔ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup walnuts
  • 4 large egg whites
  • ⅔ cup mochiko (glutinous rice flour)
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon canned sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Ingredients to make honey walnut shrimp
  3. Stir water and sugar together in a small saucepan over high heat. Bring to a boil and add walnuts. Boil for 2 minutes, then drain and place walnuts on a cookie sheet to dry.
  4. A small pot with walnuts simmering in sugar and water
  5. Whip egg whites in a medium bowl until foamy. Stir in mochiko until it has a pasty consistency.
  6. A bowl of whipped egg whites mixed with mochiko flour
  7. Heat oil in a heavy deep skillet over medium-high heat. Dip shrimp in mochiko batter, then fry in batches in hot oil until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  8. A battered piece of shrimp, frying in a large pot of oil
  9. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  10. A paper towel-lined baking sheet with fried shrimp
  11. Stir together mayonnaise, honey, and sweetened condensed milk in a medium serving bowl.
  12. A bowl of honey, mayonnaise, and sweetened condensed milk
  13. Add fried shrimp and toss to coat with sauce. Sprinkle candied walnuts on top and serve.
